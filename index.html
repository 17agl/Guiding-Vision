Welcome to the Addiction Awareness & Support

Addiction is a chronic brain disorder of reward, motivation, and memory, characterized by compulsive substance use or behavior despite harm. It combines behavioral tracking, emotional resilience, and therapeutic guidance to help individuals overcome addictive behaviors. It's managed through a combination of medical, psychological, and social support. Psychological support provides coping skills and addresses emotional factors, while educational support helps understand addiction and build relapse prevention strategies, with both being vital for recovery.

What is Addiction?

It involves long-lasting changes in the brain's reward, stress, and self-control circuits, making it difficult to stop even when the individual wants to. Addiction can involve substances like drugs or alcohol (substance addiction) or behaviors such as gambling, gaming,social media(reels) or internet use (behavioral addiction).

Types of Addiction

  • Substance Addiction (Alcohol, Drugs, Nicotine,Tobacco)
  • Behavioral Addiction (Gambling, Gaming, Internet,Shopping)
  • Food Addiction
  • Relationship Dependency

Prevention & Cure

Prevention involves awareness, counseling, healthy habits, and psychological support. Treatment includes therapy, counseling, medication, and support groups.
